How to Play

  1. Flip cards for everyone at the table by pressing Flip Cards.
  2. Keep an eye on the fruit totals. When any fruit adds up to exactly five, hit Ring the Bell!
  3. Ringing correctly scoops up the piles. Ringing early costs you the top card from your deck.
  4. Collect as many piles as you can before the table runs dry.
